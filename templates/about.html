Fuel Predict provides accurate predictions on fuel prices at service stations across NSW based off current trends.
Fuel Predict determines your nearest service station based upon your provided postcode.
Fuel Predict allows you to specify the date you want to refuel your car.


How To Use Fuel Predict

1. Choose your preferred fuel type from the drop down menu on the home page.
2. Enter your post code to find service stations closest to your current location.
3. Submit a predication date that you should like to view the fuel price prediction for.
