About Fuel Predict

Fuel Predict provides accurate predictions on fuel prices at service stations across NSW based off current trends.
By using historic fuel price history, multiple models and your provided postcode, we can determine the cheapest and
nearest service station to you. Your car tank already full? Fuel Predict allows you to specify the date you want to
refuel your car and will provide you with the best estimate for you to determine the best time to refuel your vehicle.


How To Use Fuel Predict

1. Choose your preferred fuel type from the drop down menu on the home page.
2. Enter your post code to find service stations closest to your current location.
3. Submit a predication date that you should like to view the fuel price prediction for.


Fuel Types
Ethanol 94 (E10) An ethanol-based fuel, in that it’s 10% ethanol and 90% unleaded petrol. It has a higher octane rating of 94, so it’s more
powerful than standard unleaded petrol, but the presence of the ethanol does mean it can be slightly less fuel efficient.
Premium 98 The highest octane unleaded fuel, providing higher engine power and performance as well as less pollution.
Premium 95 A type of unleaded fuel designed to be more fuel efficient and cause a smoother engine operation, improving performance.
Unleaded 91 The most common type of fuel in Australia, and can be found at pretty much every petrol station.
Diesel A fuel that is used in diesel engines and is also known as distillate.
